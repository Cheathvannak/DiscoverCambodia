Must-Have Applications

From transportation to food delivery, these apps will help you navigate Cambodia like a local

Grab

Transportation • Food Delivery • Payment

The super app for Southeast Asia. Book rides, order food delivery, and make payments all in one place. Widely available throughout major cities in Cambodia.

Key Features

  • Book tuk-tuks, cars, and motorcycle taxis
  • Order food delivery from local restaurants
  • Make payments and transfers
  • English and Khmer language support

Foodpanda

Food Delivery • Grocery Delivery

Get food, groceries, and more delivered quickly to your doorstep. Extensive selection of local and international restaurants available in major Cambodian cities.

Key Features

  • Wide range of restaurant options
  • Live order tracking
  • Scheduled delivery
  • Regular promotions and discounts
★★★★☆ 4.2

PassApp

Transportation

Cambodia's own ride-hailing service specializing in tuk-tuks and rickshaws. Provides reliable transport options with upfront pricing throughout major cities.

Key Features

  • Book tuk-tuks and rickshaws
  • Upfront fare pricing
  • Cash and digital payment options
  • Local driver network
★★★★☆ 4.1
Download

ABA Mobile

Payment • Banking

Cambodia's leading mobile banking app for local payments, transfers, and bill payments. Connect your account for seamless transactions during your stay.

Key Features

  • QR code payments accepted widely
  • Fund transfers to local accounts
  • Bill payments for utilities
  • English interface available
★★★★★ 4.7
Download

Khmer24

Shopping • Marketplace

Cambodia's largest marketplace app for finding accommodations, second-hand items, and local services. Perfect for travelers looking for longer-term stays or necessities.

Key Features

  • Housing and accommodation listings
  • Second-hand marketplace
  • Service providers and job listings
  • Direct messaging with sellers
★★★★☆ 4.0

  • Camera translation for signs and menus
