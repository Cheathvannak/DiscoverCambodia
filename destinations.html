Popular Destinations

From the majestic temples of Angkor to the pristine beaches of the south, Cambodia offers a wealth of experiences for every traveler

Siem Reap

Northern Cambodia

The gateway to the ancient temples of Angkor, Siem Reap combines small-town charm with a thriving food scene, vibrant nightlife, and traditional arts.

Highlights

  • Angkor Wat and Angkor Archaeological Park
  • Pub Street and Night Markets
  • Cambodian Circus (Phare)
  • Cambodian Cultural Village
Best time to visit: Nov-Feb
View Details

Phnom Penh

Central Cambodia

Cambodia's bustling capital sits at the junction of the Mekong and Tonlé Sap rivers and offers a mix of colonial architecture, modern development, and historical sites.

Highlights

  • Royal Palace and Silver Pagoda
  • National Museum of Cambodia
  • Central Market (Phsar Thmei)
  • Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum
Best time to visit: Nov-Feb
View Details

Sihanoukville

Southern Cambodia

Cambodia's premier beach destination offering white-sand beaches, clear waters, and a gateway to pristine islands just off the coast.

Highlights

  • Otres Beach
  • Sokha Beach
  • Koh Rong Island Day Trip
  • Ream National Park
Best time to visit: Nov-May
View Details

Battambang

Northwestern Cambodia

Cambodia's second-largest city offers French colonial architecture, a thriving arts scene, and rural countryside perfect for immersive cultural experiences.

Highlights

  • Bamboo Train (Norry)
  • Phare Ponleu Selpak Arts School
  • Bat Caves at Phnom Sampeau
  • Colonial Architecture
Best time to visit: Nov-Feb
View Details

Koh Rong

Coastal Islands

Cambodia's most beautiful island offers pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters ideal for snorkeling, and a laid-back atmosphere away from the mainland.

Highlights

  • Long Beach (Sok San)
  • Bioluminescent Plankton
  • Snorkeling and Diving
  • Jungle Trekking
Best time to visit: Nov-May
View Details

Kampot

Southern Cambodia

A charming riverside town known for its colonial architecture, famous pepper plantations, and the beautiful Bokor National Park.

Highlights

  • Bokor Mountain and National Park
  • Kampot Pepper Plantations
  • Riverside Cruises
  • Pre-Angkorian Cave Temples
Best time to visit: Nov-Feb
View Details
